# Privacy — World Cup Overlay

**No personal data is collected, stored, or shared.** No analytics, no trackers, no accounts.

- Settings live in your own Chrome account (`storage.sync`); a fixtures/scores cache lives locally
  on your device.
- The only network request is to the public sports provider **TheSportsDB**
  (`thesportsdb.com`) for World Cup fixtures and results — it contains no personal information.
- The extension never reads or transmits the content of the pages you visit. The content script
  reads only a page's hostname, locally, for the optional per-site allow/deny list.
